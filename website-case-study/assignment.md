## 2. Website Purpose and Goals

### 2.1 Purpose

The website's purpose is to provide a platform for employers and job-seekers to
connect and facilitate the hiring process.

- **Target audience:** employers and job-seeking individuals;
- **Problem or need addressed:** lack of transparency and friction before the hiring process;
- **Main purpose of the website:** to streamline the hiring process by making it easier for employers and job seekers to find the right fit.

### 2.2 Website Goals

List the main goals of the website.

1. **Goal 1:** enable employers to share and manage job positions;
2. **Goal 2:** enable job seeking individuals to search and apply for vacancies;
3. **Goal 3:** efficient matching of job-seekers and employers;
4. **Goal 4:** improve communication and follow-ups;
5. **Goal 5:** simplify and bring transparency to the hiring process.

## 3. Goal Breakdown: Actionable Steps

### Goal 1: _enable employers to share and manage job positions_
- Create business/employer registration;
- Build employer dashboard;
- Add "Job Posting" form;
- Add aplication tracking;
- Provide analytics.

### Goal 2: _enable job seeking individuals to search and apply for vacancies_
- Create job seeker registration;
- Build job seeker dashboard;
- Create job search page with filters;
- Add "Application" form for CV upload or manual description of skills;
- Provide application tracking history.

### Goal 3: _efficient matching of job-seekers and employers_
- Implement search filters;
- Add job recommendations (based on skills, location, preferences, ...);
  - _Note: AI could be a useful technology here._
- Implement feature to save jobs (later application);

### Goal 4: _improve communication and follow-ups_
- Add in-platform messaging; 
- Enable push notifications for:
  - New messages;
  - New job listings;
  - Application status updates;
  - Interview requests.
- Add email notifications.

### Goal 5: _simplify and bring transparency to the hiring process_
- Provide a clear application workflow;
- Implement application status updates;
- Build dashboard with applications overview.
